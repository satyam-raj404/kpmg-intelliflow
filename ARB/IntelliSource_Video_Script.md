# IntelliSource — Video Script
**Format:** Cinematic Motion Graphic (No camera · Animated visuals · Voiceover)  
**Duration:** 2 min 30 sec  
**Audience:** Client CPOs / CFOs / CXOs  
**Representing:** KPMG India  
**Tone:** Authoritative · Urgent · Confident · Premium  

---

> **PRODUCTION NOTES (for motion graphic team)**  
> - Color palette: KPMG Navy (#0D1F35) · KPMG Blue (#0057A8) · White · Accent Gold (#C9A84C)  
> - Typography: KPMG Sans / Gill Sans — bold section titles, clean data labels  
> - Music: Low cinematic tension build in Act 1 → confident, dynamic in Act 2–3 → resolved, premium close  
> - Data animations: Numbers count up, charts draw in, nodes connect with flowing lines  
> - Screen clips: UI screenshots of dashboards animate in as "live screens" — no actual recording needed  
> - Transitions: Cut to black between acts; logo watermark bottom-right throughout

## Narrator Direction Notes

- **Pace:** Measured, deliberate in Act 1 (let tension build). Energised in Act 2. Confident, resolved in Act 3–4.
- **Tone:** Never sales-y. This is a CFO/CPO speaking to peers — not a product pitch. Clinical authority.
- **Emphasis words:** `control` · `real-time` · `automatically` · `every single day` · `transformational`
- **Avoid:** Jargon overload, feature lists without context, superlatives without proof
- **Voice profile:** Male or female, 45–55 age bracket impression, KPMG partner-level register
